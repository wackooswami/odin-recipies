Cookie Recipie

Golden, chewy, and loaded with gooey chocolate chips, these homemade cookies are the ultimate comfort treat. Crispy at the edges and soft in the center—just one bite and you’re hooked.

ingredients:

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Line your baking sheets with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.
  2. Cream the butter and sugars In a large bowl, beat the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Mix dry ingredients In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Combine wet and dry Slowly mix the dry ingredients into the butter mixture. Stir until just combined.
  6. Add the chocolate chips Fold in those sweet chocolate chips (and nuts if you’re feelin’ fancy).
  7. Scoop the dough Drop spoonfuls of dough (about 1–2 tablespoons) onto the baking sheet, spacing them 2 inches apart.
  8. Bake ‘em Bake for 9–11 minutes, or until the edges are golden but the centers are still soft.
  9. Cool & enjoy Let them cool on the sheet for 2–3 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely (or eat one warm and melty—you earned it).
