Cake Recipie

Indulge in a rich, moist chocolate cake that's perfect for any occasion. Made with cocoa powder and topped with a silky chocolate frosting, this classic treat is every chocoholic’s dream.

ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch round or 8x8 square cake pan.
  2. Cream the butter and sugar In a large bowl, beat the softened butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla Mix in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Mix dry ingredients In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour and baking powder.
  5. Combine everything Gradually add the dry mixture to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk. Stir just until smooth—don't overmix!
  6. Pour into the pan Spread the batter evenly into your prepared pan.
  7. Bake it Bake for 30–35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Cool & serve Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely. Frost it up or keep it plain—your call!
